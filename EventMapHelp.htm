Exhibitor Listings and Search

Exhibitor Listings and Search

Displays a list of all the companies exhibiting at the event. Search for a company by clicking on a letter in the alpha-index to the right of the exhibitor list or enter a search term (the company name or a keyword)into the search box at the top. Click on a company name in the exhibitor list to display their ebooth, or click on the booth number to highlight their booth on the floor plan.

My Expo Plan

My Expo Plan

Exhibitors you save as your favorites are available in the My Expo Plan tab. Exhibitors added to your Expo Plan will show up with a red highlight around their booth.

If you did not login, a temporary cookie will be left on your computer to save your favorites for when you return. If you do login, the system will keep a permanent record of the exhibitors you saved and will allow you to access your Expo Plan from any other computer you login to.

Advanced Exhibitor Search

Advanced Exhibitor Search

Use this form to search for a company or product by keyword, categories, or location. Results will display a list of matching exhibitors, and their booths will be highlighted on the floor plan.

Exhibitor is not in My Expo Plan

Exhibitor is not in My Expo Plan

To add an exhibitor to your Expo Plan as a favorite, click on the empty star icon to the left of their name in the list. This will change the icon into a solid blue star, and the exhibitor will show up as a favorite in your Expo Plan.

Exhibitor is in My Expo Plan

Exhibitor is in My Expo Plan

If you have added an exhibitor to your Expo Plan as a favorite, they will have the solid blue star icon to the left of their name in the list. To remove them from your Expo Plan, click on the solid blue star icon. Once it has turned into an empty star outline, they have been removed from your Expo Plan.

Enhanced Exhibitor Listing

Enhanced Exhibitor Listing

A red star with an E in the middle indicates that the exhibitor has enhanced their listing with products, show specials, press releases, and/or embedded videos.

Show Maps

Show Maps

When viewing the Event Map on a phone or tablet, use this icon to switch to map view. This icon will also display a list of available maps when more than one map is available for an event.

Locate an exhibitor's booth(s) instantly on the floor plan by clicking on the booth number(s) next to their name.

Print

Print

Print the floor plan maps, the complete exhibitor list, or your Expo Plan by selecting one of the options available on the print panel.

Log In

Log In

Click to login to the system as a user or registered attendee.

Legend

Legend

Mousing over this icon opens the color legend which shows what each teardrop and booth space color means.

Home

Home

Clicking Home will open a pop-over box that displays the home page for this event.

Turn Layers On/Off

Turn Layers On/Off

Clicking the Layers tool gives the user the option to show/hide the booth colors that highlight the available/reserved/assigned status of booths, turn on/off company names for booths, show/hide favorites (My Exhibitors) tear drop on booths, show/hide the mapped booths teardrop.
